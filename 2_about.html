Our Facilities

Medi-Caps University, provides various facilities to its students. This includes a library, computer lab, hostel, conference room and auditorium. The University also has the facility of sports and gym. These facilities are established, keeping in mind the physical exercise and sports need of the students for involving them into successful personalities.

Auditorium

Medi-Caps has an open-air auditorium fit for public gatherings for more than 1500 people.

Hostel

Hostel facilities are provided for boys and girls. It is equipped with all the modern facilities for boarding and recreation facilities.

Fitness And Gym

The gym facilities are available for the students which help students in fitness.

World class library

Medi-Caps library has the latest volumes, in the fields engineering, science, technology, management and general areas. It has the membership for e-resources and has a subscription to national and international peer-reviewed e-journals.

Largest Play Ground

Caps ensures that sports are given topmost priority in extracurricular. Students regularly participate and represent the institute in various sports tournaments held in different parts of the country. The University has a well-furnished football field and cricket fields. It also has a hockey field, handball, volleyball and badminton court. Tournaments in these sports are organized in the institute at University level.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Medi-Caps has a well-maintained cafeteria situated within the institute campus premises. The food served in the canteen is prepared under hygienic conditions and are of good quality. Canteen is open from 8:00 am to 8:00 am for the benefit of students who avail the facilities of library, gymnasium after college hours.
